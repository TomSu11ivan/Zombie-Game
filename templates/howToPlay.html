Controls

  1. Press the UP ARROW to move up
  2. Press the DOWN ARROW to move down
  3. Press the RIGHT AND LEFT ARROWS to move side to side
  4. Press SPACE to shoot the zombies

Instructions

  1. For every zombie you shoot, you get one point
  2. Your goal is to get as many points as you can before you die
  3. If you get hit by a zombie once, its Game Over!
